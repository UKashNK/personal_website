# Business KPI Dashboard

##  Overview
An interactive dashboard visualizing key performance indicators for sales and profitability across regions, products, and time.
Designed to help business managers identify trends and make data-driven decisions.

##  Tools
- **Tableau**
- **Python + Plotly + Streamlit**

##  Dataset
- [Superstore Sales Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final)
- ~10,000 records of orders, customers, sales, and profit data.

##  Dashboard Features
-  **Time Series Analysis:** Sales & profit trends over time
-  **Geographic Breakdown:** Sales by region/state
-  **Product Performance:** Top 10 products by revenue
-  **Customer Segments:** Profit by segment and category
-  **KPIs Displayed:**
  - Total Sales
  - Total Profit
  - Average Order Value
  - Profit Margin %

## Interactivity
- Filters by date, region, and category
- Drill-down into product-level data
- Dynamic tooltips and visual highlights

## Results
- Identified that **West region** had the highest profit margin (22%)
- Revealed **Office Supplies** as fastest-growing category
- Enabled near real-time tracking for sales teams
